About

Study Drop is a website created to aid developing countries in need of clean drinking water whilst motivating students to succeed in their studies. For every 10 questions answered correctly, students donate a cup of water to developing countries. Although this is a prototype, we hope to collaborate with water charities willing to help with this donation.

Subject

Study Drop covers the basic principles of English and Math - more subjects will be included over time. Teachers are encouraged to have their classes complete the small quizzes on our website. The quizzes provided are meant for grades K-12, although at the moment they are provided only for grade three.
